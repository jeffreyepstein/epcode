Introduction

Welcome to the Alzheimer's Association Massachusetts/New Hampshire audio library. Publications are listed by topic. Select and click on a topic. Then click on the PLAY control arrow. The file will not play until you activate the Play control. In event of a problem, please contact our webmaster. We plan to add more audio files as they are recorded for the library. We invite you to provide feedback on this page and on Alzheimer and dementia topics you would like to see covered.
